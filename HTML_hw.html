Simple Potato Salad




Ingredients


  • 3 pounds yellow or red potatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tablespoons Dijon or yellow mustard
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 2/3 cup finely chopped dill pickles or dill pickle relish
  • 5 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste


Instructions:



  1. Bring potatoes to a boil in a large pot of salted water. Reduce to a low simmer and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until potatoes can easily be pierced with a fork.
  2. Drain potatoes and toss with vinegar. Mash gently with a fork or potato masher.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together sour cream, mayonnaise and mustard.
  4. Stir in red onion, celery, pickles, eggs and dill.
  5. Add potatoes and stir to combine. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  6. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving. Serve cold or bring to room temperature.


Enjoy!
